Privacy Policy

Policy version: GT-POL-2026.03 · Effective March 19, 2026 · Traceability details.

GovTrade Grants collects only the information required to administer applications, verify eligibility, and provide support services.

We use encryption in transit and at rest, role-based access controls, and regular audit logs for sensitive records.

To request data access, correction, or deletion, contact our support team.
