# 8-Week Ceremony Plan

## Cadence (book now)

### 1) Weekly dependency review (30 min)

- **Cadence**: Weekly for all 8 weeks.
- **Agenda**: blocker status, drift alerts, owner/action/date.
- **Participants**: PM, Engineering leads, UX lead, Legal representative, Support representative.

### 2) Eng–UX–Legal triage (30 min, twice weekly)

- **Cadence**: Twice weekly for all 8 weeks.
- **Agenda**: copy/routing/legal edge decisions plus fast approvals.
- **Participants**: Engineering, UX/Content, Legal reviewer, optional Product.

### 3) Launch readiness review cadence (Phase 5 onward)

- **Week 6**: Weekly launch readiness review (30 min).
- **Weeks 7–8**: Twice-weekly launch readiness reviews (30 min each).
- **Focus**: release blockers, signoff status, rollback readiness, comms readiness.
